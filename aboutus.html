Our Mission


Vesuvius Travel is the new innovative way to travel. Our mission is to provide the best travel experience, planning adventures that showcase the best of the world. Founders Kai Bar-on and Vidu Widyalankara created Vesuvius Travel in 2024 to share their innovative designs that shaped the travel industry. We are committed to exceeding expectations and creating a memorable travel experience for our customers that enriches their lives and leaves a positive mark on the communities we visit.
